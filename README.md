## Importance of Zero Depreciation Cover

Modern vehicles contain expensive plastic panels, sensors, headlights, bumpers, and electronic components.

Zero depreciation cover helps reduce out-of-pocket expenses during claim settlement by minimizing depreciation deductions on replaced parts.

This add-on is especially useful for:

- New cars
- Vehicles less than five years old
- Daily office commuters
- High-mileage drivers

## How Daily Driving Affects Insurance Claims

Frequent driving does not automatically increase insurance premiums, but higher exposure to road risks can increase the possibility of filing claims.

Safe driving habits may help preserve your No Claim Bonus while reducing repair expenses.

## Tips to Reduce Insurance Premium

Consider these practical suggestions:

- Renew policies before expiry.
- Avoid unnecessary claim filing for minor repairs.
- Install anti-theft devices.
- Maintain your vehicle regularly.
- Compare available coverage options.
- Choose suitable add-on covers instead of unnecessary extras.
- Drive responsibly.
